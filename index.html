About

John completed a Bachelor of Science (Genetics), a Master of Science (Bioinformatics; with distinction), and was a Research Assistant for 1.5 years in the Duchene lab. He is currently studying a Doctor of Philosophy (Medicine, Dentistry, & Health Sciences) at the University of Melbourne and is a visiting researcher at Institut Pasteur. He is passionate about computational molecular evolutionary methods, molecular clocks, and detecting rate heterogeneity/heterotachy.
